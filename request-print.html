How to Request a Print

There are three ways you can request a custom 3D print:

  1. Convert a 2D Image: If you have a 2D image and want it transformed into one of the available options (earrings, coasters, magnets, 2D print panels, pins, or keychains), provide a link to the image and specify your choice.

  2. Request a Custom Design: If you have an idea but no model or image, describe it in the form, and I will get back to you with potential design options.

  3. Have a 3D Model (.3mf) Ready: If you already have a .3mf file and need it printed, simply upload the file by providing a link in the form below.

Regardless of your choice, use the form below to submit your request, and I will reach out to confirm details. Everything is free.
